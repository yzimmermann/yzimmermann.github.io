<p>The Boston Bruins are one of the oldest and most storied franchises in the National Hockey League (NHL). The Bruins were founded in 1924 and since then have won six Stanley Cup championships and numerous other awards and accolades. The Bruins have a long and proud history of success, having won the Stanley Cup in 1929, 1939, 1941, 1970, 1972, and most recently in 2011.</p>
<p>The Bruins are a part of the Atlantic Division of the Eastern Conference in the NHL. The team is based out of Boston, Massachusetts, and plays its home games at TD Garden. The Bruins have a passionate and dedicated fan base that has been around since the team’s inception.</p>
<p>The Bruins have had a number of great players throughout their history. Bobby Orr, a defenseman who played for the Bruins from 1966 to 1976, is widely considered to be one of the greatest hockey players of all time. Other notable Bruins include Ray Bourque, Phil Esposito, Cam Neely, and Zdeno Chara.</p>
<p>The Bruins have been consistently competitive throughout their history. The team has made the playoffs in each of the last eight seasons and has won the Stanley Cup twice in that span. The Bruins have also been one of the most successful teams in the NHL over the past decade, having won the Presidents’ Trophy in 2011 and 2013.</p>
<p>The Bruins are known for their hard-nosed style of play and their commitment to defense. The team has a deep defensive core led by Chara, who is considered one of the best defensemen in the league. The Bruins also have a strong group of forwards, including Patrice Bergeron, David Krejci, Brad Marchand, and Loui Eriksson.</p>
