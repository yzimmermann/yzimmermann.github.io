<p>The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is a mixed martial arts (MMA) promotion company based in the United States. It is the largest MMA promotion in the world and features the top-ranked fighters in the sport. UFC events are held in all corners of the globe and are broadcast live on pay-per-view television.</p>
<p>The UFC was founded in 1993 by entrepreneurs Art Davie and Rorion Gracie. The original idea was to create a tournament that would determine the best martial art in the world. The first event was held in Denver, Colorado, in November of 1993. The tournament was won by Royce Gracie, a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioner.</p>
<p>Since then, the UFC has grown to become the largest MMA promotion in the world. The organization has a roster of over 500 fighters, who compete in various weight classes. The UFC has held events in over 20 countries and is broadcast in over 150 countries.</p>
<p>The UFC is a full-contact sport and allows a wide range of martial arts techniques, including striking and grappling. Fighters are allowed to use any legal technique to win a fight, including punches, kicks, elbows, knees, and submissions.</p>
<p>The UFC has several different weight classes, ranging from flyweight (125 lbs) to heavyweight (265 lbs). Each weight class has its own champion, and the UFC also has a lightweight champion.</p>
<p>The UFC also has a number of rules and regulations in place to ensure the safety of its fighters. These include the Unified Rules of Mixed Martial Arts and the Unified Rules of Combat. The rules are designed to protect the fighter and ensure fair competition.</p>
<p>The UFC has become a global phenomenon, with millions of fans around the world. It has become a mainstream sport and is now broadcast on major networks such as ESPN and Fox Sports. UFC fighters are some of the most recognizable athletes in the world, and the sport has become a multi-billion dollar industry.</p>
<p>The UFC has been a major force in the growth of MMA and has helped to bring the sport to the mainstream. It has helped to legitimize the sport and has helped to bring it to the attention of millions of fans. The UFC has become a global brand and has helped to make MMA one of the most popular sports in the world.</p>
