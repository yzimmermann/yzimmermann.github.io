<p>Tom Brady is an American football quarterback for the New England Patriots of the National Football League (NFL). He is considered one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time, leading the Patriots to nine Super Bowl appearances, winning six of them. Brady is the only quarterback to win four Super Bowl MVP awards, and he holds the NFL record for most passing yards and touchdowns in a single season.</p>
<p>Brady grew up in California, attending Junipero Serra High School, where he was a three-sport athlete. He was selected by the Patriots in the sixth round of the 2000 NFL Draft, and he made his NFL debut in 2001. Brady won his first Super Bowl in 2002, and he has since led the Patriots to seven more Super Bowl appearances, winning five of them. He has also been selected to 14 Pro Bowls, and he has been named the NFL MVP three times. Brady has also been named the NFL Offensive Player of the Year twice and the NFL Comeback Player of the Year once.</p>
<p>Brady is known for his leadership and poise under pressure. He is known for his ability to read defenses and make quick decisions. He is also known for his ability to make big plays in big moments. He has been able to do this throughout his career, and it has been a major factor in his success.</p>
<p>Off the field, Brady is known for his philanthropy. He has donated millions of dollars to various charities, including the Best Buddies Challenge and the TB12 Foundation. He has also been involved in numerous causes, such as the fight against cancer and the fight against human trafficking.</p>
