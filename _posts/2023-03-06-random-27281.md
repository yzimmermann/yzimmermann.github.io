<p>The Boston Celtics are one of the most iconic and successful basketball teams in the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Celtics have also been home to some of the greatest players in NBA history, including Bill Russell, Larry Bird, and Kevin Garnett.</p>
<p>The Celtics were one of the original eight teams in the NBA, and the team’s success began almost immediately. Led by legendary coach Red Auerbach, the Celtics won 8 consecutive NBA championships from 1959 to 1966. During this time, the Celtics were led by Hall of Famers Bill Russell and Bob Cousy. Russell was an unstoppable force in the paint, and Cousy was one of the most creative point guards in the league.</p>
<p>The Celtics continued to be a force in the NBA in the 1970s and 1980s, led by future Hall of Famers Larry Bird and Kevin McHale. Bird and McHale formed one of the most dynamic duos in NBA history and led the Celtics to 3 NBA championships in the 1980s. The Celtics also had one of the most iconic rivalries in sports history with the Los Angeles Lakers. The Celtics and Lakers faced off in the NBA Finals a total of 8 times, with the Celtics winning the championship in 5 of those series.</p>
<p>The Celtics have continued to be a successful team in the modern era, led by stars such as Paul Pierce, Kevin Garnett, and Ray Allen. In 2008, the Celtics formed a “Big Three” with Pierce, Garnett, and Allen, and the trio led the team to an NBA championship in 2008. The Celtics have also been home to superstars such as Kyrie Irving and Jayson Tatum, who have both been instrumental in helping the team reach the playoffs in recent years.</p>
