<p>John Legend is an American singer, songwriter, and actor from Ohio. He has won 10 Grammy Awards, an Academy Award, and a Golden Globe Award. He has been named one of Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People in the World.</p>
<p>Legend began his career as an underground artist, releasing several albums independently. He rose to fame in 2004 with the release of his debut album, Get Lifted. The album was a commercial success, reaching number one on the US Billboard 200 chart and selling over three million copies worldwide. The album included several hit singles, including "Ordinary People" and "Used to Love U".</p>
<p>Legend has released seven studio albums, including Evolver (2008), Love in the Future (2013), and Darkness and Light (2016). He has also released several EPs and singles. He has collaborated with a variety of artists, including Kanye West, Jay-Z, and Alicia Keys.</p>
<p>In addition to his music career, Legend has also acted in several films and television shows. He made his film debut in the 2006 film, Dreamgirls. He has since appeared in films such as La La Land (2016) and Soul (2020). He also appeared on the television series, Jesus Christ Superstar Live in Concert (2018).</p>
<p>Legend is also an advocate for social justice and civil rights. He has been vocal about his support for the Black Lives Matter movement, and has spoken out against police brutality and systemic racism. He has also advocated for criminal justice reform, LGBTQ+ rights, and women's rights.</p>
<p>Legend is married to model Chrissy Teigen, and the couple have two children together. He is an avid philanthropist, and has donated to a variety of causes, including education, healthcare, and the arts. He is also the founder of the Show Me Campaign, which works to provide educational opportunities for underprivileged youth.</p>
