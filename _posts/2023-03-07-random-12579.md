<p>The UEFA Champions League, often referred to as the Champions League, is an annual continental club football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). It is one of the most prestigious tournaments in the world and the most prestigious club competition in European football, played by the national league champions of each UEFA member association. The competition was founded in 1955 and is contested by top-division European clubs, with the winner being crowned as the European champion.</p>
<p>The Champions League is the most watched annual sporting event in the world, with an estimated global audience of over 300 million people. It is also the most lucrative club competition in the world, with total prize money of over €2 billion. The Champions League is played in a group stage and knockout format, with teams competing to reach the final, which is held in May each year.</p>
<p>The group stage consists of 32 teams, divided into eight groups of four teams each. Each team plays home and away against the other teams in their group, with the top two teams progressing to the knockout stage. The knockout stage consists of two legs, with the winner progressing to the final. The final is a single match, played at a neutral venue.</p>
<p>The Champions League is the pinnacle of European club football and is widely regarded as the most prestigious club competition in the world. It is a highly competitive tournament, with the best teams from across Europe competing for the title. The competition has seen some of the greatest players and teams in the history of the game, with the likes of Real Madrid, Barcelona, Bayern Munich, Manchester United, Juventus and Liverpool all having won the title.</p>
<p>The Champions League also provides a platform for some of the best young players in the world to showcase their talents. Many of the world’s best players have come through the Champions League, with the likes of Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, Neymar and Kylian Mbappe all having made their mark on the competition.</p>
