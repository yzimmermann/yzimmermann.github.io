<p>Bank of America was founded in 1904 as the Bank of Italy in San Francisco. It was established by Amadeo Giannini, who wanted to provide banking services to the city’s immigrant population. The bank quickly grew and expanded its services to other cities across the country. In the 1930s, it changed its name to Bank of America and began offering credit cards and other financial products.</p>
<p>Today, Bank of America is one of the largest banks in the United States, with over $2.2 trillion in assets and over 200,000 employees. It provides a wide range of products and services, including checking and savings accounts, mortgages, credit cards, investments, and more. It also offers online and mobile banking, making it easier for customers to manage their finances.</p>
