<p>Tom Brady is one of the most successful and well-known quarterbacks in the history of the National Football League (NFL). He is a six-time Super Bowl champion, a four-time Super Bowl MVP, and a three-time NFL MVP. Brady has been a part of the New England Patriots organization since 2000 and has been the team's starting quarterback since 2001.</p>
<p>Brady was born in San Mateo, California and attended Junipero Serra High School in San Mateo. He was not highly recruited out of high school and ended up attending the University of Michigan. At Michigan, Brady was a backup quarterback for his first two years before becoming the starter in his junior year. He led the Wolverines to an undefeated season in 1998 and was named the Big Ten Offensive Player of the Year.</p>
<p>After his collegiate career, Brady was selected by the New England Patriots in the sixth round of the 2000 NFL Draft. He was originally a backup to Drew Bledsoe, but when Bledsoe was injured in 2001, Brady took over and never looked back. He led the Patriots to their first Super Bowl victory in 2001 and has since led the team to five more Super Bowl victories.</p>
<p>Brady has been one of the most consistent and successful quarterbacks in the NFL for the past two decades. He has been selected to 14 Pro Bowls, has won three NFL MVP awards, and has been named the Super Bowl MVP four times. He holds many NFL records, including most passing yards, most passing touchdowns, most playoff wins, and most Super Bowl wins.</p>
<p>Brady has also been a leader off the field. He has been a vocal advocate for environmental causes, and has been active in philanthropic efforts, including the Tom Brady Foundation, which helps to support children’s health and wellness.</p>
<p>Tom Brady is one of the most accomplished and respected players in the NFL. His success on the field and his leadership off the field have earned him the admiration of fans and peers alike. Brady’s legacy will continue to be remembered for many years to come.</p>
