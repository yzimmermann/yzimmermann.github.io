<p>Juventus Football Club, commonly known as Juventus or Juve, is an Italian professional football club based in Turin, Piedmont. It is the most successful and oldest club in Italy, having won a record 36 league titles, 13 Coppa Italia titles and eight Supercoppa Italiana titles, as well as two Intercontinental Cups, two European Cups/UEFA Champions Leagues, one European Cup Winners' Cup and a joint national record three UEFA Cups.</p>
<p>Juventus was founded in 1897 and is the third oldest Italian football club. The club was a founding member of the now-defunct G-14 group of Europe's leading football clubs as well as its replacement, the European Club Association. Juventus is the only Italian club to have never been relegated from the top flight of Italian football.</p>
<p>Juventus has a long and successful history in domestic and European football. The club has won the Italian Serie A a record 36 times, the Coppa Italia 13 times and the Supercoppa Italiana 8 times. The club has also been successful in Europe, winning the European Cup/UEFA Champions League twice, the UEFA Cup three times, and the UEFA Super Cup twice.</p>
<p>Juventus has a large and passionate fan base, with over 11 million followers on Twitter, making it the most followed Italian club on the social media platform. The club has also been successful off the pitch, with the Juventus brand being the most valuable in Italian football.</p>
<p>The club has a long-standing rivalry with fellow Italian club Inter Milan, known as the Derby d'Italia. The rivalry between the two clubs is one of the most intense in world football, with both sides having won the Serie A title multiple times.</p>
<p>Juventus is also known for its youth academy, which has produced some of the best players in Italian football, such as Alessandro Del Piero, Gianluigi Buffon and Andrea Pirlo. The club has also been successful in signing some of the best players in the world, such as Cristiano Ronaldo, Paulo Dybala and Gonzalo Higuain.</p>
<p>Juventus is one of the most successful and iconic clubs in world football and its history and success make it one of the most popular clubs in the world. The club is a symbol of Italian football and is a source of pride for Italian football fans.</p>
