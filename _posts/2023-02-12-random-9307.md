<p>The Super Bowl is the annual championship game of the National Football League (NFL), the highest level of professional American football in the world. It is one of the most watched television events of the year, with millions of viewers tuning in from around the world. The Super Bowl is the culmination of a regular season that begins in the late summer of the previous calendar year. Teams compete for the Vince Lombardi Trophy, named after the legendary coach and executive of the Green Bay Packers.</p>
<p>The first Super Bowl was played on January 15, 1967, between the champion of the two rival American Football League (AFL) and the National Football League (NFL). The NFL's Green Bay Packers defeated the AFL's Kansas City Chiefs 35-10. Since then, the Super Bowl has become one of the most anticipated annual sporting events in the United States.</p>
<p>The Super Bowl is typically held on the first Sunday in February and is played between the champions of the AFC and NFC conferences. The game is usually held at a neutral site, chosen three to four years in advance. The host city is usually a large metropolitan area that has a stadium capable of hosting the event. The host city also benefits from the influx of visitors who attend the game.</p>
<p>The Super Bowl is known for its high-profile halftime show, which features some of the biggest names in music. In addition, the game is typically broadcast on multiple networks, allowing millions of viewers to watch from around the world.</p>
<p>The Super Bowl is also known for its commercials, which are often more memorable than the game itself. Companies pay millions of dollars for a thirty-second commercial during the game, making it one of the most expensive advertising opportunities in the world.</p>
<p>The Super Bowl is a cultural phenomenon that has become a part of American life. It is a celebration of the best of professional football and a time to come together and enjoy the game. The event has grown in popularity over the years and is now one of the most watched sporting events in the world.</p>
