<p>Stephen Curry is one of the most influential and successful basketball players of all time. He has been a major influence on the game since he was drafted by the Golden State Warriors in 2009. Curry is a two-time NBA MVP, six-time NBA All-Star, and three-time NBA champion. He is widely considered to be one of the greatest shooters in NBA history and has revolutionized the game with his ability to make difficult shots from long range.</p>
<p>Curry was born in Akron, Ohio, in 1988. He was a standout player in high school and college, leading his team to two NCAA tournament appearances. After college, Curry was drafted seventh overall by the Golden State Warriors in 2009. He quickly established himself as one of the best players in the league and led the Warriors to the playoffs in his first two seasons.</p>
<p>In 2012-2013, Curry had a breakout season, leading the Warriors to the playoffs and winning the NBA’s Most Valuable Player award. He averaged over 20 points per game and set a new NBA record for three-pointers made in a single season with 272. He also led the Warriors to their first playoff series win since 1975.</p>
<p>The following season, Curry led the Warriors to the NBA Finals, where they lost to the Cleveland Cavaliers. However, Curry continued to excel and was named MVP of the 2014-2015 season. He led the Warriors to a franchise-record 67 wins and was the first player in NBA history to be unanimously voted MVP.</p>
<p>In 2015-2016, Curry and the Warriors won a record 73 games and reached the NBA Finals for the second consecutive year. This time, they defeated the Cavaliers to win the championship. Curry was named the NBA’s first ever unanimous MVP for the second consecutive season.</p>
<p>The following season, Curry and the Warriors won their second consecutive championship. Curry was again named MVP and was the first player to win back-to-back MVP awards since Steve Nash in 2006.</p>
<p>Curry has continued to be a dominant force in the NBA. He has been selected to six All-Star teams and has won three championships with the Warriors. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history and has revolutionized the game with his ability to make difficult shots from long range.</p>
