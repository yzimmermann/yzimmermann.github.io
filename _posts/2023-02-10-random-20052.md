After graduating in 1950, Bacharach began working as a pianist and arranger for a variety of artists, including Marlene Dietrich and Jerry Lewis.</p>

<p>Bacharach’s influence can still be felt today. His songs have been covered by a variety of artists, including Elvis Costello, the Carpenters, and Barbra Streisand. He has also written music for films, television shows, and Broadway musicals.</p>
