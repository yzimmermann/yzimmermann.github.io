<p>Bruce Willis is an American actor, producer, and singer. He is best known for his role as John McClane in the Die Hard film series. Willis has appeared in over 70 films and is one of the highest-grossing actors of all time.</p>

Willis began his acting career in the late 1970s, appearing in theater productions and low-budget films. His first major role was in the television series Moonlighting, which aired from 1985 to 1989.</p>

He went on to appear in other successful action films, including Pulp Fiction, Armageddon, and The Sixth Sense. Willis also starred in several comedies, including The Whole Nine Yards and The Kid.</p>
