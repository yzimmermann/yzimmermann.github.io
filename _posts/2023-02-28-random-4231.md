<p>Scott Adams’ Dilbert comic is one of the most popular and beloved comic strips of all time. It has been running since 1989, and has been syndicated in over 2000 newspapers worldwide. The comic follows the daily lives of Dilbert and his co-workers, who work in a cubicle-filled office at a large, unnamed company.</p>
<p>The main character, Dilbert, is an engineer who is often seen struggling with the absurdities of corporate life. He is often seen being frustrated by the incompetence of his co-workers and bosses. His boss, the Pointy-Haired Boss, is a bumbling, clueless character who is often seen making bad decisions and coming up with ridiculous ideas. Other co-workers include Wally, a lazy slacker, Alice, a competent but jaded worker, and Asok, an eager but naive intern.</p>
<p>The comic often deals with themes such as corporate bureaucracy, office politics, and the drudgery of corporate life. It is often seen as a satire of modern office life, poking fun at the absurdity of corporate culture. Adams often uses the comic to comment on current events and social issues, such as gender inequality, environmentalism, and the rise of technology.</p>
<p>The comic has been adapted into a television series, a feature film, and a video game. It has also spawned a variety of merchandise, such as t-shirts, mugs, and plush toys.</p>
<p>The comic has been praised for its clever satire and its ability to make readers laugh. It has won numerous awards, including the National Cartoonists Society’s Reuben Award for Outstanding Cartoonist of the Year in 1997.</p>
<p>Scott Adams has said that the comic is meant to be a “therapeutic release” for people who are dealing with the frustrations of modern office life. He has said that he hopes the comic will help people to laugh at their own situations and to find humor in difficult situations.</p>
<p>Overall, Scott Adams’ Dilbert comic is a beloved and timeless classic. It has become a staple of office life, and its clever satire and humor have made it beloved by millions of readers worldwide.</p>
